About Me

Welcome to my personal webpage! I am Gayathri Srujana Manda, a dedicated and passionate graduate student currently pursuing my Master's in Data Science at the University at Albany, SUNY. With a strong foundation in Electronics and Communications Engineering and a keen interest in data science, I have developed a diverse skill set that includes programming, data analysis, machine learning, and data visualization.

In my professional journey, I’ve gained valuable experience through my role as a Data Analyst at the New York State Division of Criminal Justice Services. Here, I’ve worked on developing a comprehensive criminal justice footprint plan and creating interactive data dashboards that have streamlined processes and improved data accuracy. I also had the opportunity to intern at PricewaterhouseCoopers, where I contributed to Oracle ERP Cloud Security solutions and enhanced data security protocols. Additionally, as a Student Assistant at the ITS Service Desk at SUNY Albany, I honed my skills in IT support and customer service, resolving technical issues and improving service efficiency.

I am actively seeking job opportunities in data science, where I can apply my expertise in data analysis, machine learning, and visualization to solve real-world problems. I am particularly interested in roles that drive impact through data-driven decision-making and innovative solutions.
